Global Initiatives Department

The Global Initiatives Department at Wasatch Academy is dedicated to fostering international educational partnerships and creating opportunities for students worldwide. Through our innovative programs and collaborations, we bring together diverse perspectives and create pathways for academic excellence.

Our mission is to provide transformative educational experiences that prepare students for success in an increasingly interconnected world. We believe in the power of global education to bridge cultures and create lasting positive impact.

Dual Diploma Program

The Wasatch Academy Dual Diploma Program offers international students the unique opportunity to earn both their local diploma and a US high school diploma simultaneously. This innovative program combines:

  • Accredited US curriculum aligned with international standards
  • Flexible online learning platform with personalized support
  • Direct pathway to US higher education opportunities
  • Cultural exchange and global networking opportunities
